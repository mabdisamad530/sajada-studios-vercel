Our Story

Rooted in Faith.
Crafted with Care.

Sajda Studio was born from a simple belief — that the space where you meet your Creator deserves more than something generic off a shelf. We are a small Muslim-owned team based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, handcrafting personalised prayer mats for families across the world.

Every mat is made to order. Every name is embroidered by hand. Every box is packed with the intention that this gift will outlast any flower, any card, any candle — because it will be used five times a day, for years to come.

We started with a belief that the Muslim gift market deserved better. We are still proving it, one mat at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prices?▾
All prayer mats are $40 each. Name personalization is +$7 per mat. Adding a heart or moon symbol is +$3 per mat. Duo Sets are $80. Family Sets (4 mats) are $160. The Mat + Tesbih bundle is $50. Use code SAJDA10 for 10% off your first order.
Shipping & Delivery▾
Orders are delivered within 2–3 weeks worldwide. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships. We ship to 42+ countries at a flat rate. Expedited options are available — email us to arrange.
Personalization Options▾
Embroider any name in Arabic or Latin script for +$7 per mat. Optionally add a heart or crescent moon symbol for +$3 per mat. Choose thread color at checkout — Gold, White, or Black. For special requests email sajadastudios@gmail.com.
Returns & Exchanges▾
Because every mat is personalised specifically for you, we cannot accept returns on personalised items. If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours and we will remake and reship at no cost to you.
Bundle Savings▾
Duo Sets save on the bundle price vs ordering separately. Family Sets offer the best per-mat value at $40 per mat (vs $40 individually before personalization). The Mat + Tesbih bundle saves $10. All orders include a luxury gift box at no extra charge.
How Do I Order?▾
Click "Design Your Mat" anywhere on the site. Select your style, color, names (+$7 each), and optional symbols (+$3 each). Submit your order details and we will confirm by email within 24 hours with a secure payment link. For bulk orders of 10+ mats, email us for a custom quote.
